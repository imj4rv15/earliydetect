Leadership Team

Sheena Gill

Executive Vice-President & Chief Growth Officer

Selected as part of Washington Business Journal's Top 40 Business Leaders under 40, Sheena is a recognized public health executive who is personally and professionally passionate about advancing AI to improve health outcomes for all through early disease detection. Prior to joining CognitiveCare, she held C-Level roles with a Public Health and Health Care Delivery Consulting Organization and Senior Executive roles with an Education Technology Company. Sheena has served on multiple corporate boards, including a Health Communications firm, a start up in the Value Based Payment solutions space, and a Contract Research Organization. Prior to her executive-level appointments, she practiced law in the D.C. metro area representing hundreds of high tech companies and served in the offices of two United States Senators. Sheena is also active in D.C. 's business and charitable communities, having served on the advisory boards of TIE-DC, Rezon8 Capital, and Pratham DC

Sheena holds a B.S. in Foreign Service (in Science and Technology in International Affairs) from Georgetown University. She also holds a Juris Doctor Degree from the University of Baltimore and is admitted to the bar in Virginia, Maryland, and the District of Columbia.

Manoj Teltumbade

Research Data Scientist

Dr. Manoj Teltumbade has extensive experience in Genomics, Biostatistics, and Experimental Design. His expertise lies in applying AI/ML models to multi-omics and clinical data to gain insights into diseases at the population level.

He has a very keen interest in podcasts/ebooks related to evolutionary psychology, neuroscience, and artificial intelligence.

Manoj earned his Ph.D. in Biology with focus on Computational Genomics from CSIR-Institute of Genomics and Integrative Biology, and his M.Sc. In Biotechnology from the University of Pune. With a Merit Certificate in Machine Learning from the Indian Statistical Institute to complement his Genomics-Bio Statistics background, Manoj is amongst only a handful of world-wide scholars to clear the meritorious CSIR NET Junior Research Fellowship twice.
