Early Detect Studios

Early Detect Studios is a healthcare podcast series by MIHIC Sciences. In this podcast series we invite global healthcare industry leaders and scientists to share their views and insights on pressing problems.

Ft. Francois de Brantes, SVP - Episodes of Care at Signify Health | Early Detect Studios Episode 5
We welcome you to a riveting podcast with Francois de Brantes, senior vice president, episodes of care at Signify Health. Francois leads customer development of the Medicare Advantage, self-insured employer and commercial payer markets. As a renowned leader in the Value-Based Payments and Care world, Francois has spent close to two decades working to transform the U.S. healthcare system by improving incentives for providers and consumers in order to encourage value-based decisions.
Ft. Dr. Danielle Clark, PhD, Co-Founder and Director of ACESO | Early Detect Studios Episode 4
In this episode of Early Detect Studios (a healthcare podcast series by CognitiveCare), Sheena Gill, the chief growth officer and EVP at CognitiveCare is in conversation with Dr. Danielle Clark, Co-Founder and Director of the Austere environments Consortium for Enhanced Sepsis Outcomes "ACESO" program with The Henry M. Jackson Foundation for the Advancement of Military Medicine.
Maternal and Infant Health Crisis in the US | Ft. Dr. Rahul Gupta | Early Detect Studios - Episode 3
In this episode the founder and CEO of CognitiveCare, Venkata Narasimham Peri is in conversation with the senior vice president and chief medical and health officer of March of Dimes, Dr. Rahul Gupta. March of Dimes is the United States leading nonprofit organization that works to improve the health of mothers and babies. Dr. Gupta shares his views on maternal and infant morbidity and mortality rates in the United States, predicting future outcomes through data analytics, the potential of computational sciences AI and ML, social and lifestyle determinants in maternal health, COVID-19 and the future plans of March of Dimes.
International Women's Day - Ft. Dr. Veronica Gillispie-Bell | Early Detect Studios Episode 2
On this International Women's Day, we are honored to feature Dr. Veronica Gillispie-Bell, a board-certified obstetrician and gynecologist. Dr. Veronica Gillispie-Bell is the Senior Site Lead/Section Head, Women’s Services at Ochsner Health. In this episode, she shares her view on racial disparities and health equity and her opinion on her recent article - The Contrast of Color, Why the Black Community Continues to Suffer Health Disparities.
Dr Suresh Attili on Cancer Symptoms, Impact and Early Detection | Early Detect Studios Episode 1
On the World Cancer Day 2021, we are excited to feature Dr. Suresh Attili. Dr. Suresh is the Co-Founder and Chief Scientist at CognitiveCare and is one of India's leading medical oncologists. Dr. Attili has performed more than 280 stem cell transplants besides management of hematological disorders and all pediatric and adult solid tumors. Dr. Suresh expresses his views on the types of cancer, the genetic components of cancer, and current cancer detection and treatment measures in the healthcare industry.
